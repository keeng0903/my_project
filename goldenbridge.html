Golden Bridge

Nested at the height of 1,414m from the sea level, the Golden Bridge is no less than an iconic architectural wonder that envisages the sash-like route across the heaven. Spanning nearly 150m long in the Paradise Garden, thisbridge is regarded as a special transit stop to deliver visitors from the mountain foot or the French Village to La Jardin Dâ€™Amour Gardens.
