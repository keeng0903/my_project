Mercure Ba Na Hills French Village

As part of the 4-star hospitality chain governed by Accor Hotel, Mercure Banahills French Village Hotel is characterized by quintessentially French furnished and romantic living sphere. The hotel boasts 494 elegant suite rooms equipped with refined and well-crafted furniture. All rooms have a window looking at the French Village, which is modeled after genuine villages of France in the 19th century. Many rooms can even offer guests a panoramic view of vast ba Na mountains. In addition, Mercure Banahills French Village also meets all physical standards of a premium hotel, including a spa-gym, indoor swimming pool, bar, and retail kiosks or patisserie.

Welcome to Ba Na Hills, where all the fun begin!
